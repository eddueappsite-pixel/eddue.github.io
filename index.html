Calendar for tutors, without the clutter

edDue is an app for tutors and coaches that makes planning lessons and tracking income simpler and more efficient. Manage clients, sessions, and payments in one place — fast and clearly.

What you can do with edDue

Schedule lessons

Use the intuitive calendar to add lessons and personal events and organize your day.

Manage groups

Create student groups and mark attendance for each session.

Track subscriptions

Monitor subscription periods, session counts, and payments with ease.

Per-client currency

Assign a currency to each client — handy when students pay in different currencies.

Client balance

Track payments and outstanding balances to keep finances clear.

Stats & reports

Create reports on income, expenses, and attendance with time and client filters. Export and share with clients.

Data transfer

When changing phones, move your entire database to your new device.

Your reliable assistant

Simplify scheduling, save time, and focus on what matters — teaching and your clients’ progress.
